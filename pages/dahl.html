Dahl

Our dahl is pure comfort in a bowl. It's creamy, fragrant, full of warm spice and rich coconut - the kind of dish that makes your shoulders drop and your whole body unclench a little. This version is loaded with soft lentils, hearty chickpeas, bright scallions, fresh herbs and just enough tang to keep every bite lively.

It's nourishing, it's cozy, and it feels like the kind of food you want to eat slowly - because every spoonful tastes like care. This is plant-based cooking that feeds your body and soothes your mood at the same time.

Total Time: 30 minutes

Yield: 3 servings x1

INGREDIENTS
RECIPE SCALE :
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Chop garlic, ginger, scallions, tomatoes, and coriander stalks.
  2. Heat oil and sauté garlic and ginger for 2 minutes.
  3. Add scallions, tomatoes, coriander stalks, and salt. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in coconut milk, lentils, chickpeas, and spices.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes, then add spinach to wilt.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve with jasmine rice.
