Blueberry Scone

These bluberry scones are a soft, tender treat with just the right balance of buttery richness and juicy bursts of fruit. They have that classic bakery-style crumb that is flaky at the edges, pillowy in the center, lightly sweet and full of bright blueberry pops in every bite.

They're gorgeous with coffee, dreamy with tea and even better still slightly warm as the blueberries melt a little. Simple, elegant and quietly indulgent - the type of sweetness that makes mornings feel like a gift.

Total Time: 35 minutes

Yield: 8 scones x1

INGREDIENTS
RECIPE SCALE :
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line tray with parchment paper.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Cut in cold butter until crumbly.
  4. Add milk and vanilla. Stir gently.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Shape dough into circle and cut into 8 wedges.
  7. Bake for 18-22 minutes until golden.
  8. Cool slightly and glaze if desired.
